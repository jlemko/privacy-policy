**TempCam Privacy Policy** *Last updated: March 15, 2026* --- **Overview** TempCam is a local-only camera app. Your photos and videos never leave your device. We do not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data or media files. --- **Data We Do Not Collect** TempCam does not collect: - Photos or videos you capture - Location data - Device identifiers - Usage analytics or crash reports - Account information of any kind - Any personally identifiable information There are no servers, no accounts, and no internet connection required to use TempCam. --- **How Your Files Are Stored** All photos and videos captured in TempCam are saved exclusively in your device's internal app storage. This storage is private to the app and cannot be accessed by other apps, file explorers, or cloud backup services including Google Photos. Files are never uploaded anywhere. When you uninstall TempCam, all files stored by the app are permanently deleted from your device. --- **Automatic Deletion** TempCam includes an optional auto-delete feature that permanently removes files from your device after a user-selected retention period (24 hours, 3 days, 7 days, or never). This process runs entirely on your device with no external involvement. --- **Permissions** TempCam requests the following permissions: - **Camera** — required to capture photos and videos - **Biometric / Fingerprint** — optional, used only for local app lock authentication. No biometric data is stored by TempCam. TempCam does not request permissions for location, contacts, microphone (outside of video recording), or network access. --- **Children's Privacy** TempCam does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. --- **Changes to This Policy** If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted with an updated date. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. --- **Contact** If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: lemkolabs@gmail.com
